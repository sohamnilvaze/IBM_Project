**WHEREAS** the parties have entered into discussions and reached an understanding on the terms and conditions of this collaboration;

**ARTICLE 1: Scholarship Details**

Brilliant Solutions shall establish scholarship for students pursuing degrees in computer science at University. The scholarship program details are as follows:

* Eligibility criteria: The scholarship shall be awarded to students pursuing undergraduate or graduate degrees in computer science at University. Eligible students must hold a cumulative GPA of 3.5 or higher (on a 4.0 scale), demonstrate financial need, and be enrolled in the 2024/25 academic year.
* Amounts awarded and number of scholarships available annually: Brilliant Solutions will award ten (10) scholarships of $5,000 each during the 2024/25 academic year.
* Award considerations: Selection of scholarship awardees will be based on the recommendations of the selection committee formed by University.

**ARTICLE 2: Promotion and Selection Processes**
