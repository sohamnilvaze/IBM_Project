**Memorandum of Understanding between GlobalTech Corporation and Oakdale University**

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is made on this 18th day of October, 2024 by and between:

**GlobalTech Corporation**, a global leader in innovation and technology, with its principal place of business at 100 GlobalTech Drive, Minneapolis, MN 55402, USA ("GlobalTech");

**Oakdale University**, a reputable institution of higher learning, with its principal place of business at 100 Oakdale Drive, Oakdale, IL 60181, USA ("University");

**WHEREAS** GlobalTech and University desire to collaborate on an exchange program for students and faculty to promote mutual understanding, cultural exchange, and professional development; and

**WHEREAS** the parties have entered into discussions and reached an understanding on the terms and conditions of this collaboration;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

**ARTICLE 1: Purpose and Scope**

The purpose of this MOU is to establish a collaborative exchange program between GlobalTech and University to facilitate the exchange of students and faculty between the two parties.

**ARTICLE 2: Duration and Structure**

The exchange program shall commence on January 1, 2025 and shall continue for a period of three (3) years. During this period, the parties shall work together to develop a program structure that includes:

* A minimum of two (2) student exchanges per year, with a duration of eight (8) weeks each.
* A minimum of two (2) faculty exchanges per year, with a duration of three (3) months each.
* Mutual evaluation of the exchange program to ensure its effectiveness and to identify areas for improvement.

**ARTICLE 3: Academic Credits**

Each participating student shall receive academic credits in exchange for their participation in the program. The credits shall be awarded by the University as follows:

* One (1) credit for every five (5) hours of work completed by the student.
* The credits shall be transferable to the student's parent institution upon completion of the exchange program.

**ARTICLE 4: Support Services**

GlobalTech shall provide the following support services to the participants:

* Housing assistance for participating students and faculty: GlobalTech will provide each participant with a stipend to cover the cost of on-campus housing at Oakdale University.
* Travel assistance: GlobalTech will cover the reasonable expenses related to transportation for participating students and faculty.
* Access to GlobalTech facilities and resources for participating faculty: GlobalTech will provide participating faculty members with access to its research facilities and resources for the duration of their stay.

**ARTICLE 5: Evaluation**

The parties shall establish a joint evaluation committee to assess the performance of participants in the exchange program. The evaluation criteria shall include:

* Academic performance
* Professional development
* Contribution to the exchange program
* Cultural adaptation

**ARTICLE 6: Term and Termination**

This MOU shall commence on January 1, 2025 and shall continue for a period of three (3) years. Either party may terminate this MOU by providing a thirty (30) day written notice to the other party.

**ARTICLE 7: Confidentiality**

The parties acknowledge that this MOU contains confidential information and agree to maintain the confidentiality of all information exchanged in the course of this collaboration.

**ARTICLE 8: Governing Law**

This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Illinois and the United States of America.

**ACKNOWLEDGEMENT**

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to the terms and conditions of this MOU.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**

The parties have executed this MOU as of the date first above written.
