RECITALS
WHEREAS, Apollo Healthcare Ltd. ("AHL") specializes in healthcare services; and
WHEREAS, School of Nursing, St. John's College ("SJC") has expertise in nursing education.
ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE AND SCOPE
1.1 The purpose of this MOU is to develop specialized training programs for new medical technologies.
1.2 The scope of this MOU includes:
Curriculum development
Training schedules and locations
Certification processes
Evaluation methods
ARTICLE 2: CURRICULUM DEVELOPMENT
2.1 AHL and SJC shall jointly develop the curriculum for the training programs.
2.2 Curriculum development responsibilities:
AHL shall provide input on medical technology requirements
SJC shall provide input on nursing education standards ¹
ARTICLE 3: TRAINING SCHEDULES AND LOCATIONS
3.1 Training programs shall be conducted quarterly.
3.2 Locations:
AHL's training facilities
SJC's simulation labs
ARTICLE 4: CERTIFICATION PROCESSES
4.1 Participants shall receive certification upon completing the training program.
4.2 Certification requirements:
Attendance and participation
Written examination
Practical demonstration ²
ARTICLE 5: EVALUATION METHODS
5.1 Evaluation methods shall include:
Pre- and post-training assessments
Participant feedback
Program effectiveness review ³
ARTICLE 6: DURATION AND TERMINATION
6.1 This MOU shall remain in effect for 2 years from the Effective Date.
6.2 Either Party may terminate this MOU with 30 days' written notice.
ARTICLE 7: CONFIDENTIALITY
7.1 The Parties agree to keep confidential all information exchanged under this MOU.
7.2 The Parties shall not disclose confidential information to any third party without prior written consent.
ARTICLE 8: GOVERNING LAW
8.1 This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
8.2 Any disputes arising from this MOU shall be resolved through arbitration.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF
The Parties have executed this MOU as of the date first above written.
