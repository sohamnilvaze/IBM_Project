MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING
This Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") is entered into on this 22nd day of October, 2024 ("Effective Date"), by and between:
EcoPulse Technologies Ltd.
123, Tech Park
Bangalore, India 560100
Represented by: Mr. Vivek Kumar, CTO
Department of Engineering, VIT University
VIT Campus, Vellore
Tamil Nadu, India 632014
Represented by: Dr. K. S. Sujatha, Professor and Head
RECITALS
WHEREAS, EcoPulse Technologies Ltd. ("EPT") specializes in renewable energy solutions; and
WHEREAS, VIT University's Department of Engineering ("VIT-Engg") has expertise in research and development of innovative energy technologies.
ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE AND SCOPE
1.1 The purpose of this MOU is to establish a research partnership between EPT and VIT-Engg for the development of renewable energy technologies.
1.2 The scope of this MOU includes:
Sponsored research on renewable energy technologies
Development of innovative solutions
Knowledge sharing and capacity building
ARTICLE 2: RESEARCH OBJECTIVES AND DELIVERABLES
2.1 Research Objectives:
Develop efficient solar energy harvesting systems
Design innovative wind turbine blade designs
Improve energy storage systems for renewable energy applications
2.2 Deliverables:
Quarterly research progress reports
Bi-annual research review meetings
Final research report and presentation
ARTICLE 3: FUNDING AND PAYMENT SCHEDULE
3.1 EPT shall provide funding of ₹50 lakhs (Indian Rupees Fifty Lakhs) over 2 years.
3.2 Payment Schedule:
₹15 lakhs upon signing of this MOU
₹15 lakhs after 6 months
₹10 lakhs after 12 months
₹10 lakhs upon completion of research project
ARTICLE 4: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
4.1 Ownership: All intellectual property rights arising from the research shall be jointly owned by EPT and VIT-Engg.
4.2 Commercialization: EPT shall have the first right to commercialize any innovations developed.
4.3 Royalty: VIT-Engg shall receive a royalty of 5% on net sales of commercialized products.
ARTICLE 5: REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
5.1 VIT-Engg shall submit quarterly research progress reports to EPT.
5.2 Bi-annual research review meetings shall be conducted to assess progress.
ARTICLE 6: DURATION AND TERMINATION
6.1 This MOU shall remain in effect for 2 years from the Effective Date.
6.2 Either Party may terminate this MOU with 30 days' written notice.
ARTICLE 7: CONFIDENTIALITY
7.1 The Parties agree to keep confidential all information exchanged under this MOU.
7.2 The Parties shall not disclose confidential information to any third party without prior written consent.
ARTICLE 8: GOVERNING LAW
8.1 This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
8.2 Any disputes arising from this MOU shall be resolved through arbitration.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF
The Parties have executed this MOU as of the date first above written.
