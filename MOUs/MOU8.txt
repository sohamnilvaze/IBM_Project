**Memorandum of Understanding between Data Insight Laboratories and Oakdale University**

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is made on this 18th day of October, 2024 by and between:

**Data Insight Laboratories** ("Data Insight Laboratories"), a leading provider of data analytics solutions, with its principal place of business at 123 Data Drive, Sunny Brook, IL 60121, USA; and,

**Oakdale University** ("University"), a reputable institution of higher learning, with its principal place of business at 100 Oakdale Drive, Oakdale, IL 60181, USA;

**WHEREAS** Data Insight Laboratories and University desire to collaborate on a data-sharing arrangement to promote research and education in the field of social sciences; and,

**WHEREAS** the parties have entered into discussions and reached an understanding on the terms and conditions of this collaboration;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

**ARTICLE 1: Data Sharing Arrangement**

Data Insight Laboratories and University agree to share anonymized data for research purposes. The data to be shared shall include:

* Socio-economic data (e.g., demographic, socioeconomic status, education levels)
* Employment data (e.g., industry, occupation, job satisfaction)
* Market data (e.g., consumer behavior, market trends)

The data shall be used for research purposes only and shall not be used for any commercial or commercial-related purposes.

**ARTICLE 2: Data Security Measures and Confidentiality Agreements**

The parties acknowledge that the data provided is valuable and sensitive in nature and shall therefore take appropriate measures to ensure its security. Specifically:

* Data Insight Laboratories shall maintain a secure server environment for the storage of the data.
* Researchers from University shall establish user accounts with Data Insight Laboratories to access the data and shall obtain permission from Data Insight Laboratories before sharing the data with third parties.
* All parties agree to adhere to their respective data privacy policies and procedures.

**ARTICLE 3: Duration of Data-Sharing Arrangement**

The data-sharing arrangement shall commence on January 1, 2025 and shall continue for a period of three (3) years. Upon expiration, the parties shall consult to extend the data-sharing arrangement or terminate.

**ARTICLE 4: Acknowledgement in Research Publications**

University shall acknowledge Data Insight Laboratories as the source of the data in any publication, conference presentation, or research report that uses the data. Data Insight Laboratories shall provide a comprehensive dataset list with method appendix to facilitate future research work.

**ARTICLE 5: Acknowledgment and Compliance**
