RECITALS
WHEREAS, Nova Scientific Instruments Pvt. Ltd. ("NSI") specializes in designing and manufacturing scientific instruments; and
WHEREAS, Ravenshaw University's Department of Science ("RUS") desires to enhance its research and educational capabilities with advanced lab equipment.
ARTICLE 1: PURPOSE AND SCOPE
1.1 The purpose of this MOU is to establish a partnership between NSI and RUS for the provision of scientific instruments and related services.
1.2 The scope of this MOU includes:
Provision of lab equipment
Maintenance and support services
Training for faculty and students
ARTICLE 2: EQUIPMENT PROVISION
2.1 NSI shall provide the following equipment to RUS:
5 units of Digital Microscopes (Model DM-1000)
2 units of Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrometers (Model FTIR-5000)
3 units of Thermal Analyzers (Model TA-2000)
2.2 The equipment shall be delivered to RUS within 6 weeks from the Effective Date.
ARTICLE 3: MAINTENANCE AND SUPPORT
3.1 NSI shall provide comprehensive maintenance and support services for the equipment, including:
Regular calibration and servicing
On-site repair and replacement of defective parts
Telephone and email support
3.2 NSI shall respond to maintenance requests within 48 hours.
ARTICLE 4: TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
4.1 NSI shall provide training to RUS faculty and students on the operation, maintenance, and application of the equipment.
4.2 Training sessions shall be conducted at RUS premises within 3 months from equipment delivery.
ARTICLE 5: DURATION AND RENEWAL
5.1 This MOU shall remain in effect for 3 years from the Effective Date.
5.2 Upon expiration, this MOU may be renewed for an additional 2 years, subject to mutual agreement.
ARTICLE 6: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
6.1 NSI retains all intellectual property rights to the equipment and related technology.
6.2 RUS shall not reverse-engineer or modify the equipment without NSI's prior written consent.
ARTICLE 7: CONFIDENTIALITY
7.1 The Parties agree to keep confidential all information exchanged under this MOU.
7.2 The Parties shall not disclose confidential information to any third party without prior written consent.
ARTICLE 8: GOVERNING LAW
8.1 This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
8.2 Any disputes arising from this MOU shall be resolved through arbitration.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF
The Parties have executed this MOU as of the date first above written.
