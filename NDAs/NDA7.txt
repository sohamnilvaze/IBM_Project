**NON-DISCLOSURE AND CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT**

This Non-Disclosure and Confidentiality Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on October 20, 2024 (the "Effective Date") by and between:

**John Academy University** ("University") located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA; and,

**City Hospital and Health Services** ("Healthcare Provider") located at 456 Medical Center Drive, Anytown, USA;

(collectively the "Parties").

**CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION**

1. For the purpose of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means all data, information, and materials, including but not limited to:
	* Patient identifiable data (including but not limited to names, addresses, dates of birth, medical records, and other personal health information).
	* Proprietary analysis methods and algorithms.
	* Business strategies and plans.
	* Trade secrets.
2. The Healthcare Provider shall provide the University with access to Confidential Information for research purposes only.
3. The Confidential Information shall include but not be limited to:
	* Medical records of patients treated at City Hospital and Health Services.
	* Clinical trial data related to new treatments and medications.
	* Business strategies and policies related to patient care and management.

**OBLIGATIONS**

4. The University agrees to:
	* Keep the Confidential Information confidential and not disclose it to any third party, except as permitted under this Agreement.
	* Use the Confidential Information only for the purposes specified in this Agreement.
	* Not copy, duplicate, or reproduce the Confidential Information in any way.
	* Destroy any Confidential Information provided to the University at the end of the research project.
5. The University shall also:
	* Ensure that all University personnel who have access to the Confidential Information are aware of and agree to be bound by the terms of this Agreement.
	* Take reasonable measures to protect the Confidential Information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

	* Ensure that all patient identifiable data is properly anonymized and de-identified prior to disclosure to the University.
	* Verify the identity of all University personnel who will have access to Confidential Information.

	* The completion of the research project.
